About Us

Mercy Schools Limited, located in Abossey-Okai and Gbawe, Accra, Ghana, was founded by Mrs. Mercy Nortey in 1981 as an evening class for primary pupils preparing for the Common Entrance Examination by the West African Examination Council (WAEC). In 1983, the school added a crèche department and in 1996, a primary school department. In 2003, the school expanded beyond Abossey-Okai to include a location in Gbawe.

The school received accreditation from the Ghana Education Service in 2000 and was incorporated as a limited company in 2001. In 2013, the school opened a Junior High School department in Mataheko. The school’s motto is “Walk as Children of the Truth” and the school colors are deep blue and white. The school has successfully prepared students for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) each year and has graduated many prominent individuals.
